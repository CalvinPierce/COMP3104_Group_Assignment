George Brown College today is one of the top colleges in the greater Toronto area (GTA).
The school was established in 1967 and has 3 campuses throughout Toronto: Waterfront Campus, St.James Campus,
and Casa Loma Campus.

George Brown currently offers 202 full time programs with a wide range of courses making it an option
for everyone. On top of that, George Brown has one of the best reputations in the GTA known as a school,
that provides top tier education with real actual useful job skills.
